About ESLAB

The mission of the lab is to enhance the knowledge of sustainable architecture and urbanism through innovative educational programming and strategic research at the intersection of architectural design, engineering, computer science, and building performance simulation. Typical research outcomes of the lab are sustainable design analysis methods, daylight, energy, and thermal comfort modeling workflows, teaching concepts, design optimization and simulation software, and building component prototypes.
